Student Life

Shahriar Shahi Ishan

School life is the most pleasant period in a man’s life. Everyone longs for those cheerful days in his later life. One feels very happy when he recalls the old memories of his school life.The joys of school life are numerous. School is a place of youth. Even the old feel happy and smart in the company of young students. It is a carefree life. The student is not worried about the worldly affairs. The student’s demands are easily met by his parents. He is concerned only with his lessons and games. He has time enough to play and make his health. He remains cheerful most of the time. Some of his friends are naughty. They cut jokes with their class fellows. But there are some industries boys too. They work hard to surpass others. They cut joke with their class fellows.We pass happy evening in the playground. There are keen contests among players. They are cheered up with clapping. This is the innocent pleasure which we cannot get anywhere else.The teacher are lovable personalities. We are familiar with their particular habits. They get angry and punish but at heart, they are our well wishers. They become ideal examples for us. Discipline, cooperation and tolerance through school activities. In fact, the character is formed in the school. Thus we become good citizens and lead a successful life in future.The school activities like tournaments, picnic and other functions are the source of great joy. We pass a jolly good time at school. But we should not waste this time. The success of our future life depends on this period. The child is the father of the man. The school prepares us to fight the battle of life valiantly. We are little heroes in the making.
